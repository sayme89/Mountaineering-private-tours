Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you contact us or book a tour, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone)
  • Tour preferences, dates, and group details
  • Experience level and medical information relevant to safe guiding
  • Payment and billing information

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and process bookings
  • Plan safe and appropriate itineraries
  • Communicate important trip updates and safety information
  • Process payments and maintain booking records
  • Improve our services and customer experience

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Our guides and support staff for trip planning and safety purposes
  • Payment processors for transaction handling
  • Emergency services if necessary for your safety
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve user experience and understand site usage. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Medical and Emergency Information

Medical information provided for safety purposes is treated with strict confidentiality and shared only with guides and emergency personnel as necessary for your wellbeing during tours.

Third-Party Services

Our contact forms may use third-party services (such as Formspree or Netlify Forms). These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
